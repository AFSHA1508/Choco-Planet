About Us

Choco Planet is a home based brand established in October 2020, started simply for the love of cake.At present choco Planet is a home based store, however aims to have a global reach someday, with your support and amity.

Each piece of cake we make is specially handmade with love and precision keeping your requirement and taste in mind; to bring you the most tasty cakes at an affordable price.

From Cakes, Donuts, Cup cakes, Chocolate baskets to Mousse mugs. We provide the choicest collection of handmade Cakes to suit your every occasion, we have it all!! So hurry up and grab your personalized Cakes at affordable rates!!

For all the special occasions out there, we also make customized cakes according to your choice of taste and comfort requirement!!

We hope you enjoy shopping with us.
